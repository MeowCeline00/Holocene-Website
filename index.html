Holocene

Overview

Holocene is an adventure/pvp game set in the future where an ice age has frozen over the Earth. As the years go by and the ice starts to slowly melt again, animals have mutated, and humans have adapted to the cold. Humans call these new mutated animals "Holo's". Players can collect Holo's and use them to fight monsters and level up to fight higher level monsters as they progress through different areas of the world.
